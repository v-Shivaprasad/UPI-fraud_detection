UPI Fraud Detection 🔐

AI-powered system to analyze UPI transactions and predict fraudulent activity in real-time.

Why We Built This App

UPI has transformed payments in India, but with convenience comes risk. Fraudsters exploit vulnerabilities through scams, phishing, and unauthorized access. Our mission is to create a smart fraud detection system to help banks, apps, and users stay safe.

How It Works 🚀

  • Analyzes your transaction history.
  • Detects unusual patterns like sudden location or device changes.
  • Uses Machine Learning to calculate a fraud probability score instantly.

Why It Matters 🔐

Digital trust is the backbone of financial systems. Our app acts as a guardian for your UPI payments, ensuring safe digital transactions and preventing fraud.
